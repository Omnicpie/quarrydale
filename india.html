The Quarrydale Goes to India project

 

The Quarrydale goes to India project is a long running event that has been run by the sixth form, offering students the opportunity to go to an orphanage in India which doesn't get government funding.

 

Each year we take 6 students on the trip to India, where they spend time before hand raising money for the trip making it so they can go and also raising money for the orphanage/school.
